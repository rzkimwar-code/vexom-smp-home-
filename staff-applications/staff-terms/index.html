Before You Accept

These terms apply to anyone accepting an Administrator or Developer position on Vexom SMP. By accepting your role, you are agreeing to everything listed on this page. If you are unsure about any of these terms, please speak to Lukeantqueen or Alta before accepting your position.

These rules exist to protect the fairness and integrity of the survival experience for all players. They are not optional — violations will be taken seriously.

1. Survival Mode Restrictions

Upon accepting an administrator or developer role, you forfeit your right to play survival mode on the server. This is to prevent any form of admin abuse, intentional or accidental.

Specifically, you may not:

  • ⛔ Kill other players using your privileges or in-game advantages
  • ⛔ Build or place blocks in any survival world (world, world_nether, world_end)
  • ⛔ Use /give on any player who is not also an administrator or developer
  • ⛔ Use creative mode to introduce illegal items into the survival economy — this includes anything obtainable through the operator items tab or via /give
  • ⛔ Use any staff command for personal gain, to benefit friends, or to disadvantage other players

2. Console Access

If a head administrator grants you permission to access the server console, the following applies:

  • ✅ You may only use console access for legitimate server administration tasks
  • ⛔ You may not hold operator status in-game for extended periods while console access is active
  • ⛔ You must deop yourself in-game as soon as you are finished using the console
  • ⛔ Console access must never be used to modify player inventories, world data, or server settings without explicit approval from a head administrator

3. Conduct & Professionalism

As a staff member, you represent Vexom SMP. You are expected to maintain a professional and respectful standard of conduct at all times, both in-game and on the Discord server.

  • ✅ Treat all players fairly and equally regardless of your personal relationships with them
  • ✅ Keep staff-related discussions and server information confidential unless told otherwise
  • ⛔ Do not share your operator credentials, console login, or any admin tools with anyone not on the staff team
  • ⛔ Do not abuse your position to settle personal disputes with other players

4. Availability & Communication

Staff members are expected to remain reasonably active. Going inactive without notice is not acceptable.

  • ✅ Notify a head administrator in advance if you expect to be away for more than 7 days
  • ✅ Respond to staff pings on Discord within a reasonable timeframe
  • ⛔ Disappearing without communication for more than 14 days may result in your role being reviewed or removed

5. Resignation

If you choose to resign from your staff position, the following will occur immediately upon resignation:

  • ⚠️ Your ender chest and inventory will be cleared
  • ⚠️ You must wait 31 days before you are permitted to play on the server again as a regular player
  • ⚠️ All operator permissions and console access will be revoked immediately
  • ⚠️ You may not re-apply for a staff position for 60 days following your resignation

These measures exist to prevent staff members from using their access to stockpile items or information before leaving.

6. Termination

Failure to follow any of these terms may result in immediate termination of your staff position, and will likely result in a permanent ban from the server.

Head administrators reserve the right to terminate any staff member at their discretion if the integrity of the server or its community is at risk. Termination decisions made by Lukeantqueen or Alta are final.

Termination follows the same inventory and waiting period rules as resignation, with the addition that re-application may be permanently denied depending on the severity of the violation.

By accepting your staff role on Vexom SMP, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to all of the above terms.
